About

ZooBot is based on tycoon games. Everyone has their own zoo, you can expand your zoo by buying fields (sections), animals and more! Everyone will receive an "hourly payment" every 60 minutes exactly on a whole hour.

The amount of "hourly payment" you will receive depends on the animals you have, if you have multiple or expensive animals you will receive more money. You will need to buy fields (sections) to buy animals on.

When you have saved enough money you will be able to prestige. If you prestige everything will be reset execept for the multiplier. After you have prestiged everything will be multiplied with your multiplier.

The final goal is to be the highest on the leaderboard or have the best looking zoo.

General commands Args: ( ) = optional [ ] = must Description
!profile (tag) Shows your profile
!buy field [field number] Buy a field (section).
!buy animal [animal name] Buy an animal for your field (section).
!sell field [field number] Sell a field (section).
!sell animal [animal name] Sell an animal.
!bal (tag) Shows your balance.
!animals (number) Shows the layout of the animals, cost and hourly payout.
!fields Shows the layout of the fields and cost.
!prestige Level up!
Admin commands Args: ( ) = optional [ ] = must Description
!prefix [newprefix] Changes the prefix.
!set announce [on/off] Sets the hourly announcement on or off.
!set channel [ID] Sets the hourly announcement channel.
!set current Shows the current settings.
